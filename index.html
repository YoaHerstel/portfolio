introduce myself

I am a 25-year-old graphic designer from the Netherlands with a passion for sustainability and creating stunning designs. With a Bachelor's degree in Industrial Product Design, I've always focused on the graphic aspect of the field. As the founder of Liquid Jungle, I've designed logos for local startups and hostels around the world. I'm particularly proud of my recent NFT project, where I drew 1000 unique NFTs while managing a successful Twitter and Discord community of over 2000 followers. I'm excited to continue exploring new ways to express my creativity and bring value to clients through design.

For this internship I designed a remote-controlled hand for picking up objects in toxic environments. Utilized SolidWorks for 3D printing and created wooden prototypes for testing. Collaborated with my team on the remote control mechanism for finger movement.

Sense up

As part of a school project, I collaborated with two team members to design a sensor that attaches to the front of a shoe and sends a notification to a phone when uneven terrain is detected. This solution aimed to improve mobility and prevent falls for older individuals.

Delivery Bike

We designed a cargo bicycle for local supermarkets, featuring a detachable front for sustainable transportation. This design reduced truck use, facilitated loading/unloading, and made it easy to navigate busy or narrow streets.
